    • ABOUT US

    We started from the steeling trading business, exporting and importing steel between Cambodia and China.

    Cambodia's geographical and economic potential were eye catchers for our board of directors to consider investing in the heart of the economic zone of this small country.

    In addition with the strong relationship between the governments of the two countries, thus we decided to establish "Huale Steel".

    MISSION

    Our objective is to manufacture and offer the highest quality products to our clients while using sustainable techinques that adhere to the highest worldwide environmental control requirements. We believe that profoundly skilled and driven workers are the key to achieving our goals, and we will continue to provide excellent training and investment in their growth.

    VISION

    Our plan of action is to continually participate in extending our generation limits and increasing our production capabilities with the explicit goal of meeting the epidemically growing global demad and therefore maintaining our position at the leading edge of the steel industry.
